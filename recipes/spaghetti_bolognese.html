Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich meat sauce served over spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is softened.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti noodles and enjoy!
